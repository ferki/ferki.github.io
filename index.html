Welcome, I am glad you are here!

What I find most rewarding professionally is to enable others to improve their workflows. This usually means taking quality standards or execution speed to the next level. Often both. Therefore my services typically include lots of on-the-job training.

I offer services in English or Hungarian around the following topics:

  • RexOps: commercial support for Rex, the friendly automation framework
  • Modern Perl: modernizing legacy code or starting new projects from scratch
  • DevOps: solving challenges around IT infrastructure, automation, and software delivery
  • engineering management: (re)organize workflows for efficiency and effectiveness
  • open source: open sourcing projects, managing community and developer relations
  • inner source: accelerate your organization by adopting open source best practices

Please follow the links above for further details. I would be happy to chat, and learn more about how I may contribute to your success!

Where?

I am based in Berlin, Germany, but also often staying at various other places. For this reason I primarily offer remote services. At the same time, I am happy to arrange occasional on-site presence whenever that is the best course of action.

When?

I follow workdays according to the local calendar wherever I stay at the moment. My standard working hours are between 09:00 and 18:00 according to my local time.
