How to get started?

Since each goal and situation is different, I prefer to work together on finding an agreement that fits the most important one: yours. The typical workflow is the following:

  1. Book an appointment to chat about how I may contribute to your success.

  2. Discover all the crucial details together. If no project brief is available yet, I’m glad to create one with you during a workshop.

  3. Get a personalized offer based on the project brief.

Where?

The overwhelming majority of my work is de facto remote. I also travel often from my base in Berlin, Germany, which allows me to flexibly choose a location that fits any given situation the best.

When?

My standard working hours are between 10:00 and 18:00 on workdays according to my local timezone and calendar.

How much?

My typical remote service fee is €850 per day.

On-site service and work outside standard working hours are subject to availability, surcharge, and/or travel expense reimbursement.

My standard fees are reassessed periodically to ensure fair offers and a sustainable service quality on a constantly changing market.

The above rates are correct at the time of publication, and exclude any VAT which may apply. Discounts for long-term or pay-in-advance orders may be discussed. Errors and omissions excepted.
